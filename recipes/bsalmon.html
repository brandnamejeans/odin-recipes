Blackened Salmon

Description

Salmon coated in spices and blackened in butter.

This is usually how I cook salmon.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of salmon, skin on
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chili powder
  • dash of cumin
  • 1 tbls of butter
  • lots of paprika

Steps

  1. Blend salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin, and paprika in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in skillet to medium high heat
  3. Cover salmon with spice blend
  4. Put salmon in skillet spices down
  5. Cook for 12 minutes
  6. Flip salmon over to skin side
  7. Cook 12 more minutes
