Avocado Toast

Description

A piece of toast with smashed avocado spread on it.

This recipe can be traced back to at least a few days ago.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • half of an avocado
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • dash of cumin
  • 1/8 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 egg
  • butter

Steps

  1. Throw bread in toaster
  2. Throw butter in skillet
  3. Fry egg in skillet
  4. Mash avocado with lime juice, salt, pepper, cumin, and chili powder
  5. Throw toast on plate
  6. Smear the avocado goo on toast
  7. Cook egg to desiredness and put on top of toast
  8. Eat avocado toast
